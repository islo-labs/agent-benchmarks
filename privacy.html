Privacy Policy

Last updated April 21, 2026

Agent Benchmarks is a free, public resource. We don't have accounts, we don't collect form submissions, and we don't sell data. This page explains what we do collect and who helps us do it.

What we collect

When you visit this site, we automatically collect standard usage data:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL and UTM parameters
  • Browser type, OS, and device category
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Clicks on outbound links

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any information you type — because there's nowhere to type anything.

Analytics — PostHog

We use PostHog to understand how people use the site — which sections get read, which links get clicked, where people drop off. PostHog processes data on US servers. You can review their privacy policy at posthog.com/privacy.

Visitor identification — RB2B

We use RB2B to identify companies visiting the site. RB2B may use cookies and similar technologies to associate your visit with other information — including your email address — held by RB2B or its data partners.

When you visit this site, cookies and similar technologies may be used by our online data partners or vendors to associate these activities with other personal information they or others have about you, including by association with your email. We (or service providers on our behalf) may then send communications and marketing to these email addresses. You may opt out of receiving this advertising by visiting app.retention.com/optout.

To opt out of RB2B's GDPR data collection: rb2b.com/rb2b-gdpr-opt-out.

Cookies

PostHog and RB2B set cookies to track sessions and identify returning visitors. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. The site works fine without them.

Data sharing

We don't sell your data. We share data only with the service providers listed above (PostHog, RB2B), and only to operate this site.
